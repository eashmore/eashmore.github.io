Here's A Bit About Me!

How I Got Here

After graduating from the University of California, San Diego with a degree in Environmental Science, I landed a job at a chemistry lab, analyzing various food products for compliance with FDA standards. It was soon after, that I realized I was heading down a career path I did not wish to be on. Repetitive lab tests and mundane lab reports were all what greeted me here. The only highlight of my work was when a test failed. It became like a logic puzzle; what was wrong with the product and why? This one bright light quickly faded however as these tests became more like clockwork; less of a puzzle and more of an exercise in memory. It was around this time I began looking for a way out. I remembered back to a intro to CS course I took in college. I remembered how much I had enjoyed writing and manipulating code and how, frankly, it all just made sense. So I decided to give programming another go, this time starting with JavaScript.

Where I'm Going

Now, several months later, I have completed App Academy, an immersive, full stack, web development course with an emphasis on coding style and best practices and an acceptance rate of less than 5%, and have built multiple web apps on Rails, Backbone.js and Ember.js. I want to find a team that can facilitate my desires to grow as a programmer and write better code. A team that expects nothing less from its engineers and will provide them with the opportunities needed to improve themselves. I want to be taken out of my comfort zone, either through a deepening of my understanding of my current skills and knowledge base, or through new languages and frameworks, which I can generally pick up rather quickly. But what I want most of all, is to find a team that is passionate about their product and their vision, so that I may be impassioned along with them.
